Desktop Support Specialist

TechTent is seeking a Desktop Support Specialist to join the IT team in Example, Sydney. Position provides technical support to sales representatives and requires professionalism, initiative, and a demonstrable aptitude for technical topics.

Reference number: 47373

I am the Text

Position Title: Desktop Support Specialist

Succeessful Position Title: Executive Desktop Support Specialist


General Skills

Communication skills Microsoft Exchange Operating systems System design CompTIA A+ LAN Analysis skills DNS DHCP

Primary Duties and Responsibilities:

  • Resolve helpdesk requests
  • Manage IT hardware and software resources
  • Create and maintain software images
  • Build and install new hire workstations

Minimum Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Technology, Engineering or equivalent practical experience.
  • Practical understanding of IP addressing basics (including DNS and DHCP).
  • Basic network configuration and troubleshooting skills.
  • Familiarity with Windows 7 and Microsoft Office.
  • Working knowledge of PC hardware.
  • Knowledge of imaging software.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Knowledge of Active Directory / Microsoft Exchange
  • Strong analytical and problem solving abilities
  • Ability to collaborate and work across geographically dispersed functions.
  • Ability to work efficiently and independently without constant supervision
  • Data analysis and data-driven decision-making skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal business communication and presentation skills.
  • Excellent project management skills and attention to detail.

Salary

Salary range typically falls between $38,823 and $51,851. Salary ranges can vary widely depending on many important factors, including education, certifications, additional skills and languages, and years of experience


Required documents for the Position Application

To be considered, please upload the following documents:

  1. Copies of resume
  2. A cover letter
  3. Qualifications and Certificates ( e.g: Bachelor of Computer Science, etc)
  4. Copies of your professional portfolio or clips
  5. Recommendation letters ( if any)
